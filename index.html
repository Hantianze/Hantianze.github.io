matters needing attention

  • Tie a rope

    There are several ways to tie a visitor to a rope - put a harness around your body and tie your ankles, legs or arms

  • Lift start

    You must sit firmly before the lift starts. Don't tie the bungee jumping rope before the lift starts, otherwise the rope will be easily wound into a ball.

  • Equipment safety

    Make sure all equipment can be used safely before jumping.

  • Don't drink

    Don't go bungee jumping after drinking. Alcohol will not only damage your judgment

  • Make sure the rope

    Make sure the rope hangs out in a way that allows you to bounce safely. If the rope is hooked or entangled, you may be injured.

  • History of heart and brain diseases

    Make sure that rope bungee jumping requires high physical quality. People with a history of heart and brain disease cannot participate
